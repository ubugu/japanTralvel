The following is a suggested half day itinerary for northern Kyoto, including the popular templesKinkakuji and Ryoanji. This itinerary can be easily extended to a full day plan with a few additions.

Kinkakuji (45 minutes)

Start at Kinkakuji. Go early in the day when it is less crowded.

Ryoanji (1 hour)

Take bus number 59 to Ryoanji and spend some time in contemplation at Japan's most famous Zen garden. Alternatively, it takes about 20 minutes to walk from Kinkakuji to Ryoanji.

Ninnaji (1 hour)

Walk a few more hundred meters to Ninnajiand check out its interesting temple grounds. Afterwards, consider continuing on toArashiyama from nearby Omuro-Ninnaji Station
